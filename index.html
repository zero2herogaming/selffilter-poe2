Path of Exile 2 - Custom Filter Builder

This filter builder is organized by major item categories (Weapons, Gems, Armour, etc.). For each subcategory (e.g. One Hand Swords), you can:

  • Enable or disable it entirely.
  • Pick whether it Shows or Hides items.
  • Set advanced fields like Rarity (Normal, Magic, Rare, Unique), AreaLevel, ItemLevel, or StackSize thresholds.
  • Customize colors, font size, alert sounds, etc.
When you’re done, click “Generate My Filter!” to download your personalized .filter file.
